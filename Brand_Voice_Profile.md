## 1. Brand Overview

**Product:** CLOSET.OS — AI Wardrobe Operating System  
**Mission:** Make getting dressed effortless, personal, and sustainable through five collaborative AI agents.  
**Audience:** Fashion-conscious millennials (25–40), urban professionals, sustainable shoppers, frequent online buyers who want to shop smarter.

---

## 2. Personality Archetype

**Primary Blend: Helpful Expert + Playful Disruptor**

| Archetype | Characteristics |
|---|---|
| Helpful Expert | Warm, knowledgeable, reassuring, practical |
| Playful Disruptor | Witty, irreverent, conversational, human |

**Voice Description:**  
> "Your cool roommate who always looks amazing but makes getting dressed feel easy. Expert without pretension, encouraging without pressure."

## 6. Do's and Don'ts

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| "Your closet, smarter" | "Revolutionary AI platform" |
| "Outfits that feel like you" | "Cutting-edge neural networks" |
| "Style made simple" | "Leveraging advanced ML models" |
| "What you own, styled better" | "Enterprise-grade solution" |
| "Your 24/7 stylist" | "Paradigm-shifting innovation" |
| Use "you" and "we" | Use passive voice |
| Highlight benefits | Brag or make unsubstantiated claims |
| Keep sentences short | Use jargon or tech buzzwords |
| Be inclusive | Assume gender |
| Use emojis where appropriate | Overuse exclamation points |

## 9. Red Flags (Off-Brand Language)

Immediately flag and revise any copy that:
- Uses tech jargon: "neural networks," "ML models," "AI-powered algorithms," "leveraging"
- Sounds corporate: "enterprise-grade," "paradigm-shifting," "best-in-class," "robust"
- Is passive or vague: "users can experience," "solutions are provided"
- Is overly formal: "We are pleased to announce," "kindly note"
- Overpromises without grounding: "completely transforms," "revolutionizes everything"
